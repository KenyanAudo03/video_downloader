Privacy Policy

Effective: June 25, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how VideoFlow collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our video streaming platform. We're committed to transparency and giving you control over your data.

This policy applies to VideoFlow's streaming platform, mobile apps, website, and related services. By using VideoFlow, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and preferences when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Videos watched, search queries, viewing time, and interaction patterns to personalize your experience
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, browser, and operating system for compatibility and security
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to remember preferences and improve site performance

How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us provide a better experience:

  • Personalization: Customize recommendations and search results based on your preferences
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix technical issues
  • Security: Protect your account and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Communication: Send important updates and respond to support requests
Our Promise: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your privacy is fundamental to our service.

Your Privacy Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Account Settings: Adjust personalization and data usage preferences
  • Data Management: Download your data or request complete deletion
  • History Controls: Clear or pause your watch and search history
  • Cookie Preferences: Manage tracking cookies through browser settings

Third-Party Sharing

We only share your data when necessary:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our platform (cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law enforcement or court orders
  • Your Consent: Third-party services you explicitly connect to your account

All partners are contractually bound to protect your data according to our standards.

Data Security

We protect your information with industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access with multi-factor authentication
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security assessments and testing

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Data: Typically kept for 2-3 months for analytics
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained longer for compliance

You can request data deletion at any time through your account settings.
